## Streamline your operations with our amazing customer portal

Our customer portal is designed to make your life easier by allowing you to log in and view the status of your loads, make comments, and view your billing portal. You can also interact with our dispatchers and get real-time insight without having to call or email us.

### Key Features

- View the status of your loads and outstanding bills
- Interact with our dispatchers for real-time insight
- Make comments on your loads and update important notes
- Download documents for past loads
- Expedite payments and streamline your operations

We understand that not every company is able to integrate fully with their forwarder, which is why we provide a secure and controlled way for you to access and share data. Our customer portal is a win for everyone as it expedites communications and payments while ensuring that everyone feels safe and informed.

## Experience the Benefits of Real-time Communication

By using our customer portal, you can rest assured that you are not being misled by truckers, and they do not have to worry about too much customer service. The interactions are in real-time, which makes them very safe. You can update important notes for orders such as contact information, ready and eta times, and our dispatchers can view and download documents for past loads, eliminating the need for constant phone calls and emails.

We believe that our customer portal is a game-changer for the transportation industry, and we invite you to experience the benefits of real-time communication with MyCartage.
