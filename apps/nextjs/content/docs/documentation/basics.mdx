## On a mission

> We are here to build and support you for the long haul. We want to facilitate the communication layers for everyone involved in the trucking industry to make things easier, friendlier, and less stressful. We know how hard the people in our industry are working every hour of every day and we find extreme joy in making their lives easier and more productive and more satisfying.

## Features

It comes with the following features out of the box:

1. GPS support that just plain works.
2. GPS tracking - Notifications if drivers are not expedited.
3. Shared data with customers - let them trust you.
4. Customer portal - let them have access, securely.
5. Data Security with advaced Technology.
6. Incredibly safe Azure B2C Authentication.
7. Modern dispatching systems.
8. iOS and Android applications.
9. Smarter Invoicing and Accounting.
10. API integration & "No Code" integration.
